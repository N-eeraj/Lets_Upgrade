Iron Man

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark was a billionaire industrialist, a founding member of the Avengers, and the former CEO of Stark Industries.

Thor

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, the former king of Asgard and New Asgard, and a founding member of the Avengers.

Captain America

Captain Steven Grant "Steve" Rogers is a World War II veteran, a founding member of the Avengers, and Earth's first known superhero.

Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff, also known as Scarlet Witch, is a native of Sokovia who grew up with her fraternal twin brother, Pietro.

Doctor Strange

Doctor Stephen Vincent Strange M.D., Ph.D is the sorcerer and a Master of the Mystic Arts, then later became the Sorcerer Supreme.

Spider-man

Peter Benjamin Parker is a high school student who gained spider-like abilities, fighting crime across New York City.
